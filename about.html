Welcome To Bharathbrands

The Best IT Solution With Experts

Welcome to Bharathbrands, where innovation meets purpose! We are a dynamic IT software development and servicing company dedicated to revolutionizing industries by addressing critical challenges and providing cost-effective software solutions. At Bharathbrands, we believe in the power of technology to transform businesses and enhance operational efficiency.

Established in 2017, Bharathbrands Limited stands as a leading software development company with a strong presence in Kenya and India, renowned globally for its exceptional innovations in the realm of software solutions. With a rich legacy spanning two decades, Bharathbrands has been a trusted partner in delivering innovative solutions to the Healthcare, Banking & Finance, and Government Institutions.

Our Values

With Bharathbrands, you can make work faster, easier, and more human.

In the Healthcare sector, we provide comprehensive solutions such as Hospital Information Management System (HIMS), Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS), Medical Drug Distribution System, Patient Care System, and Health Information Exchange Platforms, along with an Online Doctors Platform.

Zero-Code Platform: The zero-code platform offered is highlighted as a revolutionary approach to creating and automating services without the need for coding expertise. It emphasizes flexibility by being technology-agnostic, seamlessly integrating with any tech stack or platform in use.

Addressing the needs of Government Institutions, Bharathbrands provides solutions in Revenue Management, County Service Requests Management, a platform for digitizing physical files into an Electronic Document Management System, and Electronic Know Your Customer (EKYC) systems.

Our dedication to delivering purpose-driven solutions and our extensive experience make Bharathbrands a trusted partner for organizations seeking reliable and tailored software solutions across various industries.
