Bharathbrands Global Expansion Strategy

Bharathbrands embarked on a strategic global expansion journey, underlining our commitment to providing innovative IT solutions on a worldwide scale. Our expansion timeline is marked by key milestones as we ventured into diverse markets, shaping our operational dynamics for optimum efficiency and customer satisfaction.

INDIA (2017):

Founded in 2017 in the vibrant landscape of India, Bharathbrands set its sights on creating innovative solutions for industries. The journey commenced with the ambitious development of an e-commerce platform, laying the foundation for future endeavors.

Bharathbrands remains committed to its mission of providing innovative and comprehensive IT solutions, continually adapting to industry needs and pushing the boundaries of technology.

INDIA - THE HUB FOR DEVELOPMENT AND SUPPORT

Bharathbrands strategically positions India as the central hub for product development, cutting-edge research, and robust customer support. India serves as the factory for all our innovative solutions, leveraging a talented pool of professionals and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Additionally, it acts as the global backup for the company's operations, ensuring resilience and continuity.

KENYA (2021):

In 2021, Bharathbrands identified Kenya as a pivotal location, strategically positioned to serve as the sales hub for the African continent. The decision to establish a presence in Kenya underscores our commitment to expanding our reach within the African market, recognizing its unique challenges and opportunities.

KENYA - SALES HUB FOR AFRICA:

Acknowledging the substantial growth potential in the African market, Bharathbrands designates Kenya as the sales hub for the entire continent. This strategic move allows us to intimately understand and respond to the unique needs and demands of the African market. Our presence in Kenya facilitates direct engagement with local businesses, ensuring personalized solutions and fostering enduring partnerships.

Bharathbrands remains unwavering in its pursuit of global excellence, and our expansion strategy is not just about reaching new horizons but about creating a lasting impact on the industries we serve. As we continue to grow, our commitment to innovation, customer-centricity, and adaptability remains at the core of our global endeavors.
